# 1. Business Problems 

#### Description

---

**Rossmann** is a real German drugstore whose business model is the sale of products such as medicines, baby and body care, hygiene, sun protections, cosmetics, dental hygiene, household, pets, hair care, perfume, fragrances, and food products. 

**Purpose:** to forecast sales revenue of the next 6 weeks for Rossmann.
